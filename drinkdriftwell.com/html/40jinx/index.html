meet the 40 jinx

Pop Up

notification overload

The Jinx is called Pop-Up, it lives on your phone. What was that notification? The answer must be known​.

​ Hear him buzz and ring as he orchestrates his song​ with ever growing messages of “Hey”,“Watch This”, and “It’s been too long!”

Binge Beast

are you still watching?

This Jinx is called Binge Beast, and it comes in many forms. We all have a favorite, do tell me what’s yours?

 For some Reality TV really helps them unwind, but when there’s a bit too much it’s the Binge Beast you’ll find.

Hop-To-It's

the endless to-do list

Your boss says “good morning, here’s something for you”, and you eagerly add a bullet to the list marked “to-do”.

 With a stroke of the pen, you’re sealing your fate. Here comes Hop-To-It, the Jinx that seem to procreate.

Internet Black Hole

mindless browsing

The internet black hole is the sneakiest of Jinx. It hides in plain site, in the form of internet links.

 Before you know it, you’re lost in the void. As hours pass, watching videos of cat clips you thought you enjoyed.

Scrōl

social media devotion

Scrōl will show you your friends, and all that they do. He’ll show you celebs, some influencers too!

 He’ll show you it all, as you get lost in the glare. Leaving you wound up, weary, and worse for the wear.

Nellies

the sunday scaries

The Nellies feed on anticipation. They rack your nerves with no hopes of cessation.

 It’s Sunday, they taunt you about all that is due! When the Nellies inquire, your Sundays are through.

Coulda, Woulda, Shoulda

spinning, spiraling, and second guessing

Coulda, Shoulda, Woulda invade each endeavor, and prevent the opportunity to think through things better.

 I coulda done this, I woulda done that, I shoulda done more, why did I just stand pat?

Burnout

All work, no play

If your job keeps you up all hours of the night​, and you haven’t slept well in at least a fortnight​.

 There’s a Jinx to blame, please pardon the Cliché​. It’s the Jinx named Burnout, whose motto is “All work, No Play”.

11th hour

Save it for later

Something needs doing but you don’t have the power?​ Your procrastination might just be the Jinx 11th Hour​.

This Jinx causes you to put off what can be done​. Creating more stress as the only outcome.​

Mortif-eye

Self doubt

Trying to relax? Let’s think about that one thing that you said​, the Jinx named Mortif–Eye will never let it get out of your head​.

 Anything embarrassing or uncomfortable will do​. Nothing’s off limits when this Jinx grabs a hold of you.​

Gimme Gimme

Lack of self control

Gimme Gimme is the Jinx with demands​. It always wants more, so it barks out commands​.

 Making it hard to resist those unhealthy temptations​. When Gimme Gimme arrives, say goodbye to moderation​.
